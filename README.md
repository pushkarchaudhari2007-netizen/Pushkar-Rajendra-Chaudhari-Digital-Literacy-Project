## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on creating awareness about **Digital Literacy** among students. It includes a visually engaging infographic and supporting documentation to help learners understand essential digital skills required in today’s world.

Digital literacy is important for effective communication, safe internet usage, and building a professional online presence.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- To explain the concept of digital literacy in a simple way  
- To highlight useful digital tools for students  
- To promote safe internet practices  
- To guide students on maintaining a professional online presence  
- To introduce proper email etiquette

## 🚧 Challenges Faced
One of the main challenges was balancing content and design. Adding too much information made the infographic crowded, while too little reduced clarity. This was solved by using short and clear points.
